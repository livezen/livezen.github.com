OSHO

Until just a few years ago a being was among us who made every possible effort to explain what Zen really means – and not only Zen, but all the sayings of those unique souls who went to the very depth of our human-ness.


He has devised many new meditation techniques and used traditional eastern methods as well as modern therapeutic approaches to create awareness in his disciples. The immensity of his contribution to the world of consciousness will reveal itself only in the times to come.













OSHO left his body in 1990. His daily spontaneous talks in English and Hindi have been recorded in more than 600 books; many of those have been translated in various languages.
